About Baruah Constructions

  • Baruah Construction was started in 1993 by Mr. Anil Baran Barua, a Civil Engineer with North East Frontier Railway, after his retirement from service. Ever since its inception, customer satisfaction and timely completion of its projects has been the prime motto of the organization.

    Today Baruah Construction is an ISO 9001:2008 Organization. We have completed many successful projects and also many projects are under development. We strive for excellence, and aim not only to meet our customers' expectations but to surpass them. Our goal is to provide each client with quality, professional home designs, house plans and more from leading designers and architects.
    Baruah Constructions' primary business is development of residential, commercial and retail properties. The company has a unique business model with earnings arising from development and rentals. Its exposure across businesses, segments and geographies, mitigates any down-cycles in the market.

    Since its inception in 1993, Baruah construction has completed many projects successfully at Guwahati .
